About Paiyur Group

A Legacy of Natural
Fruit Processing

Founded in 1996 by Dr. E. Madhavan, Paiyur Group began as a small local venture in the fertile Krishnagiri Belt — today it stands as one of India's most respected names in fruit pulp manufacturing.

We specialize in producing high-quality fruit pulps, purees, concentrates, spices, sauces, and jams, with an unwavering emphasis on natural ingredients and hygienic processing. Our products are packed in both Canned and Aseptic formats to meet diverse industrial requirements worldwide.
